About Brothers Painting

Brothers Painting is committed to professional conduct, integrity, and quality paint work. We have a lot of experience in residential and commercial painting, interior as well as exterior. Our goal is to do the job right so that your home is protected and will last. Jesse Fellers has been painting in the Kansas City area since 2001 after moving here from Montana. Brothers Painting started in 2010 when as a partnership was formed between Jesse and Rob with over 34 combined years of experience.
